Ross Garwood

Hats can keep me warm in winter, and help me block out the sun in summer. Hats with different designs and styles can show my different personality and style. I always feel happy when people appreciate my hat and say something nice to me.

"Mr. Bowtie" is me! "Mr.Bowtie" is a cute nickname given to me by friends around me. Although some bow ties in my collection are similar in color and pattern, I still carefully choose the right bow tie to match the outfit and occasion. I am always grateful to my friends for giving me bow ties as gifts to fill my collection.

IT IS WHAT IT AIN'T!

Repairing and maintaining cars is my other hobby. I often talk to my friends and relatives about the difficulties encountered in car repairs and how to better maintain the car. I get a lot of help in exchanging and sharing experiences with people, and I also helped many friends repair their cars.
